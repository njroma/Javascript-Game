Bug 404

Level

This website is a Parody of Base 404 with a developer-themed twist and a custom game. The game challenges you to collect bugs while avoiding hidden bombs. You have 3 lives, and each bomb you hit costs one. Multiple difficulty levels add to the challenge.

"Hello Future Web Developers, Let's find the bugs in fix this website"


To Win: Find the 🐞 hidden in the boxes and avoid the 💣 bombs.

Note: The default level is Entry Level. Choose your level for challenges.

Warning: You only have 3 lives. Use them wisely. Good luck!

To Start: Press the Start button!
